# Canvas Design Workflow

Follow this two-step process for all static visual art and posters:

## STEP 1: DESIGN PHILOSOPHY CREATION (.md file)

Create a VISUAL PHILOSOPHY (not layouts) interpreted through:
- Form, space, color, composition
- Images, graphics, shapes, patterns
- Minimal text as visual accent

### THE CRITICAL UNDERSTANDING
- **MANIFESTO**: First, write an art movement manifesto (4-6 paragraphs).
- **CRAFTSMANSHIP**: Stress that the work is meticulously crafted by a master.
- **VISUAL COMMUNICATION**: 90% visual, 10% essential text. Text is a visual anchor.

### HOW TO GENERATE A VISUAL PHILOSOPHY
1. **Name the movement** (e.g., "Chromatic Silence").
2. **Articulate the manifesto** (4-6 poetic, philosophical paragraphs about space, form, scale, and hierarchy).

---

## STEP 2: CANVAS EXPRESSION (.pdf or .png file)

Execute the philosophy on a single-page canvas.
- **MUSEUM QUALITY**: Use repeated patterns, perfect shapes, and systematic reference markers.
- **MINIMAL TEXT**: Whisper-quiet or bold gestures. Proper margins are non-negotiable.
- **AUTHENTICITY**: Must look like it took hundreds of hours. No overlapping elements.

---

## OUTPUTS
1. `design_philosophy.md`: The Manifesto.
2. `canvas.pdf` or `canvas.png`: The Art piece.
